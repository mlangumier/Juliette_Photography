Experiment: Art as a teacher

Experiments, whatever shape or form, serve as a teaching tool. It is one of the most satisfying teaching technique thanks to its work of research and discovery that - with a sense of curiosity that will never be quenched - brings us as many answers as it brings questions, and leaves us in a constant state of hunger for this adventure of creation.
That state of mind convinced me to merge passion and work in order to share my knowledge and my curiosity for research and creation; hoping to exchange with those who, like me, are looking for a new way to experience art - and photography in particular.
